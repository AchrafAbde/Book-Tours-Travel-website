Highlights

✓ Explore Fez, Morocco's oldest imperial city

✓ Take a jeep safari through the iconic red dunes

✓ Travel by camel across the red Moroccan desert

✓ Meet nomadic families and learn about their way of life

✓ Enjoy a show of musicians and traditional folk dancers

  • DAY 1 Marrakech
    Welcome to Morocco! After arriving at the airport, transfer to your hotel and meet your guide and fellow travelers at 7:00pm. Spend the evening exploring Marrakech or join an optional group dinner.
  • DAY 2 Marrakech, Essaouira
    Begin with a tour of Marrakech’s key sites including Koutoubia Mosque and Bahia Palace. En route to Essaouira, visit an Argan Women Cooperative. Upon arrival, explore the charming UNESCO-listed Medina.
  • DAY 3 Essaouira, Casablanca
    Travel north to Casablanca, Morocco’s largest city. Visit the impressive Hassan II Mosque and other highlights like United Nations Square. Enjoy a photo stop by Rick’s Café from the classic film.
  • DAY 4 Rabat, Chefchaouen, Tetouan
    Journey along the coast to Rabat for a guided tour of the capital’s historical sites. Continue to the picturesque blue city of Chefchaouen before staying overnight in Tetouan.
  • DAY 5 Tetouan, Chefchaouen, Fes
    Explore Chefchaouen’s medina and stunning views before heading to Fes. Known for its vibrant souks and leather tanneries, Fes is a cultural heart of Morocco.
  • DAY 6 Fes
    Dive into Fes’s rich history with a guided tour covering major sites including the Royal Palace and Quaraouiyine Mosque. Enjoy dinner and rest up for the journey inland.
  • DAY 7 Fes, Merzouga
    Travel through the High Atlas Mountains to the desert village of Merzouga. Experience a camel trek at sunset and enjoy a night under the stars in a desert camp.
  • DAY 8 Merzouga
    Start the day with a sunrise over the dunes, followed by a 4×4 jeep safari exploring the desert. Relax by the pool, sand-board, or climb the dunes in the afternoon.
  • DAY 9 Merzouga, Marrakech
    Leave the Sahara and drive back to Marrakech. Stop at Ourzazate movie studios and Tichka mountain pass. Arrive in Marrakech and explore Djemaa el-Fna Square, known for its vibrant market atmosphere.
  • DAY 10 Marrakech
    Enjoy your final breakfast, bid farewell to your travel mates, and check out of the hotel by 11:00am. Look forward to your next adventure with Travel Talk!
