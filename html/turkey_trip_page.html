Itinerary

✓ Start and end in Istanbul! With the In-depth Cultural tour Absolutely Turkey, you have a 7 days tour package taking you through Istanbul, Turkey and 16 other destinations in Turkey. Absolutely Turkey includes accommodation in a hotel as well as an expert guide, meals, transport and more.

  • DAY 1 Istanbul - Ankara
    Depart from Istanbul to Ankara, crossing the intercontinental bridge. Arrive in Ankara for dinner at the hotel. Overnight: Ankara Meals: Dinner
  • DAY 2 Ankara - Cappadocia via Salt Lake
    Explore Ankara, visiting the Museum of Anatolian Civilizations and Ataturk Mausoleum. Travel to Cappadocia, stopping at an underground city en route. Overnight: Cappadocia Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • DAY 3 Cappadocia
    Tour Cappadocia’s unique landscapes, including the fairy chimneys, Goreme Valley, Uçhisar, and Derbent valley. Visit artisan workshops. Overnight: Cappadocia Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • DAY 4 Cappadocia- Sultanhani - Pamukkale
    Depart for Pamukkale, stopping at the Sultanhan Caravanserai. Enjoy free time at Pamukkale's hot springs and travertine pools. Overnight: Pamukkale Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • DAY 5 Pamukkale – Ephesus – İzmir
    Visit Ephesus, exploring its ancient ruins and the House of the Virgin Mary. Continue to İzmir, stopping at a leather production center. Overnight: İzmir Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • DAY 6 zmir - Pergamon – Troy – Çanakkale
    Travel to Pergamon to visit the Asclepion ruins, then continue to Troy to explore its archaeological sites. Proceed to Çanakkale. Overnight: Çanakkale Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • DAY 7 Gallipoli – AnzacCove – Istanbul
    Cross the Dardanelles Strait to visit Gallipoli’s WWI battlefields. Return to Istanbul in the evening, with drop-off at your hotel. Overnight: N/A Meals: Breakfast
