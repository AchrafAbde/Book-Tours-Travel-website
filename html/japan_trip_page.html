Highlights

✓ Spend a night in an incredible mountain monastery

✓ Visit the sacred island of Miyajima and see the torii gate

✓ Take a walk in the geisha district of Gion

✓ Soak in the natural hot springs in Hakone

✓ Savour local Japanese food and travel by bullet train

  • DAY 1 Osaka
    Arrive at any time in Osaka. To make the most of this vibrant city, consider booking pre-accommodation. In the evening, meet your CEO and group for a welcome moment.
    Accommodation: Holiday Inn Express Osaka City Centre - Midosuji (or similar).
  • DAY 2 Osaka/Kōyasan
    Travel to Mount Koya, the heart of Japanese Buddhism. Ascend by train, funicular, and bus to the mountaintop temple. Enjoy a traditional shojin ryori dinner and relax in a yukata.
    Accommodation: Sekishoin (or similar).
    Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
    Transport: Train (3 hours)
    Included Activities: Overnight in Mount Kōya Temple
  • DAY 3 Kōyasan/Hiroshima
    Observe morning prayers and have a vegetarian breakfast. Travel to Hiroshima, a city marked by history, via bus, train, and funicular.
    Accommodation: Toyoko Inn Hiroshimaeki Shinkansenguchi Ⅱ (or similar).
    Meals: Breakfast
    Transport: Bullet train (5 hours)
    Included Activities: Oku-no-in Cemetery Visit
  • DAY 4 Hiroshima
    Visit the Peace Park and Memorial Museum in Hiroshima. In the afternoon, take a ferry to Miyajima Island to see the famous floating Torii Gate. Optionally, hike around Mt Misen.
    Accommodation: Toyoko Inn Hiroshimaeki Shinkansenguchi Ⅱ (or similar). Transport: Ferry to Miyajima
    Included Activities: Hiroshima Park and Peace Memorial, Miyajima Island Visit
  • DAY 5 Hiroshima/Kyoto
    Travel to Kyoto and explore the city. Visit Nijo Castle and its gardens, and take an evening walking tour of the Gion geisha district.
    Accommodation: Prince Smart Inn Kyoto Sanjo (or similar).
    Transport: Bullet train (1.5-2 hours)
    Included Activities: Gion Walk, Nijo Castle and Gardens Visit
  • DAY 6 Kyoto
    Visit the Fushimi Inari Taisha shrine and its iconic torii gates. Enjoy free time to shop, stroll along the river, or visit other temples in Kyoto.
    Accommodation: Prince Smart Inn Kyoto Sanjo (or similar).
    Included Activities: Fushimi Inari Taisha Shrine Visit
  • DAY 7 Kyoto/Hakone
    Travel to Hakone and take a boat ride on Lake Ashino-ko, hoping for a view of Mt Fuji. Enjoy a traditional kaiseki meal and soak in the onsen. Accommodation: TKP Hotel & Resort Rekutore Hakone Gora (or similar).
    Meals: Dinner
    Transport: Bullet train (3 hours), local bus (30 minutes)
    Included Activities: Lake Ashino-ko Boat Ride, Traditional Kaiseki Meal
  • DAY 8 Hakone/Tokyo
    Travel to Tokyo, arriving before noon. Spend the day exploring the city's diverse neighborhoods, parks, and shopping areas.
    Accommodation: Shinjuku Washington Hotel (or similar).br Meals: Breakfast
    Transport: Bullet train (2 hours)
  • DAY 9 Tokyo
    Depart at any time. Consider booking additional nights to further explore Tokyo's vibrant culture and attractions.
    Meals: None
  • DAY 10 Marrakech
    Enjoy your final breakfast, bid farewell to your travel mates, and check out of the hotel by 11:00am. Look forward to your next adventure with Travel Talk!
